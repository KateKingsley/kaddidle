Antheraea polyphemus

Commonly known as a Polyphemus moth, it was found on my neighbor's porch after hurricane Irene.

  

About a month after spinning a cocoon, she wiggled out one evening. It was windy and raining so I kept her overnight and let her go in the morning. I put her back on the tree she fell out of as a moth. She hung out until evening.
