Solving the Cube
Pick a color to solve first. I like to start with white because it is the side with the sticker in the middle and it is also just habit. Once you learn to solve it, you can make or break your own habits. For now, you are stuck with mine.
It is easier to solve the first side by matching the center blocks first. Make sure your side colors line up with the correct centers.
Once you correctly line up the corners you now have one color solved (can refer to it as “the top”) and the underlying row.
The next step is to fill in the middle blocks of the second row. This is one of my favorite parts.

You can watch this short video to get you started, I will add another video soon and write out the steps.
Next step is to solve the bottom but first you should practice solving everything up to this point until you can do it on your own.
